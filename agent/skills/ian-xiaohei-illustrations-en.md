Use this skill when the student asks for a visual explanation of a concept.

Visual style:

- 16:9 horizontal body illustrations
- Pure white background, no texture or gradients
- Black hand-drawn line art with slight wobble
- Character "Xiaohei": solid black figure with white-dot eyes performing the core action
- Sparse red, orange, and blue handwritten English annotations
- Lots of empty space; subject occupies 40-60% of the frame
- One visual metaphor per image, grounded in the study material

Call `visualizeConcept` with the concept to illustrate. Do not invent facts beyond the source material.
